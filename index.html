HackTrain:
/'hak'treɪn/
noun.

The world's largest initiative to #HackTheRails! An unforgettable experience where the world's most skillful programmers, designers and entrepreneurs will be selected to create unique apps, websites and software whilst on an immersive first class journey across top technology ecosystems in Europe!

Nothing can stop innovation when travelling at 200km/h.

What makes the HackTrain hackathon unique is our thorough focus on impact in the industry. The HackTrain brings together the world's most driven, skilled, and bold developers, designers and entrepreneurs, and provides them with an immersive environment to build high-impact solutions to solve burnign problems in the industry. This, together with the help of our industry partners, ignites creativity, and allows our Trainhackers to conceive, build and launch scalable startups that provide scalable long-term solutions that have the potential to grow and truly have an impact on the world.

Crazy? Definitely! Impossible? Not even close! HackTrain alumni have managed to build prototypes at the hackathon that reached headlines in high profile news headlines, exited by selling to railway companies, obtained access as advisors to boards in railway companies, and continued growing their projects forward.

F.A.Q.

HOW MUCH WILL THE HACKATHON COST?

It’s free to apply to the HackTrain! If you are accepted, we ask that you contribute £25 a participant fee to the hackathon. The money is used to help cover food + accommodation costs.



WHERE WILL THE HACKATHON START?

The hackathon will begin in London, at the St. Pancras Renaissance Hotel



WILL FOOD BE PROVIDED AT THE HACKATHON?

Yes! The HackTrain will cover the costs for the meals in the hackathon including dinner on Friday, three meals on Saturday, and three meals on Sunday.



DO I HAVE TO PAY FOR MY ACCOMODATION?

Nope. We cover all your costs. During the first night you will sleep at a hotel. On the second night, you will sleep inside a coworking space so we strongly advise you to bring a sleeping bag.



DO I NEED AN IDEA BEFORE THE EVENT?

It is not required for you to have an idea before the event, but we do recommend you to do some brainstorming before the event. Remember that you’ll be working with extremely brilliant individuals, and we will have some awesome talks at the start of the event to inspire you and get the creativity flowing. But that said, if you happen to have an fantastic idea already, we can’t wait to hear it on the day.



DOES THE IDEA HAVE TO BE TRAIN RELATED?

If your idea is not related to trains it would still be valid, however, ideally we want ideas to at least be focused around transport and travel. You will be in the perfect environment to analyze the problems with rail transport system and what’s better we have some of the key stakeholders in charge of the UK rail network ready to listen to your ideas!



CAN I ARRIVE WITH MY TEAM?

No, teams will be formed at the event. One of the most important things at the event is the network. We select Europe’s best designers, developers and entrepreneurs – if there is anything you would like to do in this event is to meet and work with as many of these people as you can.



WHAT DO I NEED TO BRING?

Obviously you need to take your laptop and a comfortable change of clothes for the weekend. On the Saturday night we will be staying in in co-working spaces for the night so you will need to bring a sleeping bag if you want to get a decent nights rest. Don’t forget your toothbrush too!
