40 hackers, 4 cities - 3 days to build revolutionary startups, whilst hacking on a train!

An Experience Like No Other.

HackTrain is the first ever train hackathon in Europe! An unforgettable experience where 40 of the best developers from all around Europe will revolutionize the world! Jump on board the HackTrain for a once in a lifetime journey, travel to the top tech spaces in the UK, hang out with the market makers in the Startup ecosystem, and create startups that will revolutionize the world!

Thanks to our strong partnership with the largest train companies in the UK, including East Midland Trains and Virgin Trains East Coast, hackers will be turning pizza into software whilst on an executive First Class Journey!

Startups, apps, prototypes will be created, and presented in front of CEOs, investors, serial entrepreneurs and evangelists - all with the objective of becoming the 2015 HackTrain champion! With TrainHackers from UK, Russia, Romania, France, Poland, Czech Republic together with many other European countries, this hackathon will certainly promise a unique experience!

Crazy? Definitely! Impossible? Not even close! Many individuals rocked our past events, creating mind blowing and revolutionary concepts! Some of our past events include Startup Bus UK 2014, Startup Weekend Moscow, Startup Hacks.

THE JOURNEY


27th March - The Launch +

10:00 - HackTrain Launch (London) +

The journey will begin with a massive launch event in London - a morning event together with the top players from the London tech and startup ecosystem, great speakers and incredible surprises. Even if you will not participate in the HackTrain, this is an event you do not want to miss!


13:30 - Pitches on the HackTrain +

Hackers, designers and entrepreneurs will hop on the HackTrain and have an opportunity to pitch their epic ideas whilst on the road to Sheffield to one of the top coworking spaces in the UK. Team formation workshops will take place, and by the time we arrive to Sheffield, everyone will be ready to ROCK!


16:00 - Arrival at Sheffield +

Amazing speakers and incredible mentors will receive all the TrainHackers at Sheffield with open arms. Teams will receive a few crash-courses and mini-workshops on things that will be essential to aid success throughout the rest of the event, and even throughout their entrepreneurial career!


18:00 - Dinner is Served +

Dinner will be served at the coworking space in Sheffield. Healthy options will certainly be available, but, like always, pizza and drinks will be there!


19:00 - Hacking Begins +

Once teams are set, hacking will begin! New gadgets and awesome tech will be released for hackers to play around. Experienced mentors will help the teams on anything that is required to ensure the best!


18:00 - Midnight Surprise & Workshops +

We have loads of surprises planned for the whole journey at the train hackathon! You will not want to miss any of these!


28th March - All on board the HackTrain +

09:00 - HackTrain to York +

TrainHackers will be jump in the Hacktrain to head to the beautiful city of York! Like always, we will have a few surprises and mentors on board to maximize the short journey!


15:00 - Tech Workshops +

We will be making sure that only the best technology is available for all the TrainHackers at the journey! Wearable gadgets like Google Glass, Oculus Rift, between many others will be available, and any extra equipment you might require can certainly be requested - we'll do our best to get everything you need to rock this event!


13:00 - Hacking at the Museum +

If you think this just can't get any more epic, the National Transport museum has allowed us to host our hackathon inside their premises! TrainHackers will be building software inside the most prestigious historical trains! How cool is that!


20:00 - HackTrain to Edinburgh +

Everyone will hop on board the HackTrain once again on the road to Edinburgh to one of the best coworking spaces in the city! Upon arrival, mentors will be ready to help out in anything required!


23:00 - All-nighter awesomeness! +

More surprises will be available for the hackers to make it through the last night! It will be an amazing, exhausting and rewarding adventure!


29th March - Final Day Begin

08:00 - Early Breakfast +

Last day of the event! Massive breakfast will be provided - fuel will be required for this intense and rewarding day!


10:30 - HackTrain to York!

TrainHackers will be getting ready for another journey to rock the finals of the HackTrain event! The finals and closing event will take place at the national York Train museum!


14:30 - Tech Check on Rails!

Tech check will begin! TrainHackers will be able to make sure that their technology works correctly, and their demos work as expected. This will be your chance to ensure a perfect final presentation!


17:00 - Final Presentations!

Final presentations will begin! TrainHackers will be showing the mind blowing startups they created throughout the intense journey.


17:00 - Announcements + Closing!

The winners of the 2014 HackTrain will be announced! Prizes will be given to the winning teams, as well as final announcements and closing words. After the announcements we'll all depart home, with an insane but rewarding experience.
