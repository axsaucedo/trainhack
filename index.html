27th March - 29th March

The most insane competition in Europe.

TrainHacks is the first ever train in Europe! An exrteme, out of this world hackathon where the top developers from all around Europe will come together to revolutionizing the world whilst travelling to the top tech cities in the UK!

A massive launch party, an unforgettable journey, incredible individuals, and uncountable mind blowing surprises are just a few of the quirks that the HackTrain will have on board!

Prototypes will be created and presented it in front of CEOs, investors, serial entrepreneurs and evangelists - all with the objective of finding the 2015 HackTrain champion! With attendees from UK, Russia, Romania, France, Poland, Czech Republic together with many other European countries, this hackathon will certainly be an unforgettable experience!

Crazy? Maybe! Impossible? Not even close! Many individuals created Revolutionary Concepts in our past Startup Bus 2014, Incredible StartUps at our Startup Weekend in Moscow and Mind Blowing Hacks at our Summerhacks in Romania.

This is your opportunity to take the leap into the amazing world of hackathons! Come join us in this incredible journey - we assure you that you won't regret it!
